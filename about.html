About INSL

Intelligent Network Systems Laboratory (or INSL), led by Professor Jeongho Kwak, is with the Department of Information and Communication Engineering (or ICE), Daegu Gyeongbuk Institute of Science and Technology (or DGIST). INSL's research focus lies in developing algorithmic and practical solutions of important networking problems, their performance and analysis over cloud computing and wireless networks. Recent research efforts in INSL have been quite diverse, ranging over multiple resource allocation (compute/storage/network), reinforcement learning and elastic content caching in edge computing architecture.
